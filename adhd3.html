            📋 Prescriber Guide — Stimulant Authorities in Australia
            
          

          


            
There are two separate authorities when prescribing ADHD stimulants: (1) State or territory authority to legally prescribe stimulants, and (2) PBS authority for subsidy — to make it cheap.


            
State-by-state

            

              
StateRequirements

              
                
NSWWrite CA2023 on every stimulant script. NSW pharmacies won't dispense without it.

                
VIC, QLD, WA, SA, ACT, TASPsychiatrists can prescribe without a permit and without CA2023.

                
SA (extended)If S8 is intended for more than 2 months, obtain individual patient authority via SA SafeScript.

                
NTApply for authority via NT Government Health. Approval lasts 2 years.

              
            

            
You don't need individual patient authority provided you stay within the state maximum, prescribe for what the state allows, and the patient is not drug dependent. Generally this is just ADHD, except WA allows more diagnoses. For other indications, apply for individual patient authority through that state's SafeScript.


            
PBS Authority — How to Get It

            
Go to PRODA → HPOS → My Providers → Authorities → Request Authority. You need your authority script authorisation number (next to your prescriber number on eScripts). You need a new authority for every new script.

            
In the Authority Request, enter the total tablets per day and the total repeats you'll actually prescribe. Vyvanse needs individual authority for each capsule strength — re-prescribing still needs a new request.

            
For patients on 5+ tablets/day of dex IR or methylphenidate IR, the 20-day rule means they may run out. Enter 200 tablets (not the default 100) to get authority for 2 bottles. 200 is the maximum for methylphenidate; dex has no legal limit but be careful above 200.


            
Special Indications

            
    
              
  • Narcolepsy (dexamfetamine): Obtain individual patient authority via SafeScript — CA2023/psychiatric powers don't apply. PBS is available for narcolepsy but requires individual authority first.
    • 
              
  • Binge eating disorder (Vyvanse): Requires individual patient authority on SafeScript. Not PBS approved — private script only.
    •
